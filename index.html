Our Story

A small salon with a big heart, in the heart of San Jacinto.

Edith's began as a one-chair shop on Sto. Thomas — a place for neighbors to drop in, share gossip, and walk out feeling like the best version of themselves. Years later, the chair count has grown, but the welcome hasn't changed.

Every service here is built around one idea: that beauty rituals should feel like care, not transactions. Whether you're prepping for a wedding or just need a Tuesday afternoon to yourself, we've saved a seat for you.
